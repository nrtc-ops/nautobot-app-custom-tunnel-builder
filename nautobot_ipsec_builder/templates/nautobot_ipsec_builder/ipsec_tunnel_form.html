What this does

Submitting this form will enqueue a Nautobot Job that connects to the selected device over SSH and pushes the following IOS-XE constructs:

  • crypto ikev2 proposal
  • crypto ikev2 policy
  • crypto ikev2 keyring
  • crypto ikev2 profile
  • crypto ipsec transform-set
  • crypto ipsec profile
  • interface Tunnel<N> with VTI protection
  • copy running-config startup-config

Device credentials are read from the environment variables NAUTOBOT_DEVICE_USERNAME and NAUTOBOT_DEVICE_PASSWORD.
